Our Solutions

Grid -Tied Solar system

The most popular option for homes. In grid tied systems, home is still connected to the power grid.if the electricity needs goes beyond the production through solar panels , power grid covers the difference

Off-Grid

Off-grid systems work independently of the power grid but have batteries which can store the solar power generated by the system.

Hybrid System

This is the middle ground option between grid-tied and off-grid. This has a has an energy storage component, like most off-grid systems, but maintains a connection to the power grid.

The system operates on power generated using solar PV (photovoltaic) system. The photovoltaic array converts the solar energy into electricity, which is used for running the motor pump set. The pumping system draws water from the open well, bore well, stream, pond, canal etc.
