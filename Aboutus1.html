Who We Are

Arraycom (India) Limited is an ISO 9001:2008 certified company and one of the leading System Integrators in India active in the field of Professional Electronics encompassing Satellite Communications, Signal Intelligence, Surveillance, Terrestrial Communications, Broadcast, Networking, Software Development and also Roof Top Grid based Solar Systems for residential and Commercial applications (MNRE and GEDA Approved).
